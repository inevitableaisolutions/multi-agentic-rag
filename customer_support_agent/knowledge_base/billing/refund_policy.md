# Refund Policy

## Overview

CloudSync Pro offers refunds under specific circumstances. Our goal is to ensure customer satisfaction while maintaining fair billing practices. All refund requests are reviewed within 3 business days.

## When Are You Eligible for a Refund?

You may be eligible for a refund in the following cases:

- **First-time subscribers**: Full refund available within 14 days of your initial subscription purchase. No questions asked.
- **Annual plan cancellation**: Prorated refund for unused months if you cancel within the first 30 days of an annual subscription.
- **Service outage**: Credit or refund if CloudSync Pro experiences downtime exceeding the SLA guarantee of 99.9% uptime in a given month.
- **Duplicate charges**: Full refund for any verified duplicate billing. These are typically resolved within 24 hours.
- **Unauthorized charges**: Full refund after identity verification for charges you did not authorize.

## When Are Refunds Not Available?

Refunds are not issued in these situations:

- Monthly subscriptions after the current billing period has started (you retain access until the period ends)
- Annual plans after the 30-day cancellation window
- Add-on purchases (additional storage, premium integrations) after activation
- Accounts terminated for Terms of Service violations

## How to Request a Refund

1. Log in to your CloudSync Pro account
2. Navigate to **Settings > Billing > Support**
3. Click **Request Refund** and fill out the form with your reason
4. You will receive a confirmation email with ticket number (format: REF-XXXXXX)
5. Our billing team reviews requests within 3 business days

Alternatively, email billing@cloudsyncpro.com with your account email and reason for the request.

## Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days. Credit card refunds appear on your statement within 1-2 billing cycles depending on your bank. PayPal refunds are typically faster, appearing within 3-5 business days.
