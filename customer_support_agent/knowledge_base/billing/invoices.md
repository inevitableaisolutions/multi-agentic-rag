# Invoices and Receipts

## Accessing Your Invoices

All invoices are automatically generated and available in your account:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Billing > Invoice History**
2. You will see a list of all invoices sorted by date (most recent first)
3. Each invoice shows the date, amount, payment status, and plan details
4. Click **Download PDF** to save a copy, or **View** to see it in the browser

Invoices are also emailed to the billing contact address on file within 24 hours of each successful charge.

## Invoice Details

Each CloudSync Pro invoice includes:

- Invoice number (format: `INV-2026-XXXXXXXX`)
- Billing date and payment date
- Plan name and billing period
- Number of licensed users
- Per-user cost and total amount
- Any add-on charges (extra storage, premium integrations)
- Applicable taxes and tax ID (if provided)
- Payment method used (last 4 digits of card)
- Company name and billing address

## Adding Tax Information

To add your company's tax ID (VAT, GST, EIN, etc.) to invoices:

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Tax Information**
2. Enter your tax ID number and business registration details
3. Click **Save**

Tax IDs appear on all future invoices. To update past invoices with your tax information, contact billing@cloudsyncpro.com with the invoice numbers.

## Requesting Custom Receipts

If you need a receipt with specific formatting or additional information (e.g., purchase order numbers, department codes), contact our billing team:

- Email: billing@cloudsyncpro.com
- Include your account email, invoice number, and the specific changes needed
- Custom receipt requests are fulfilled within 2 business days

## Bulk Invoice Downloads

Enterprise customers can download all invoices for a date range as a ZIP archive from **Settings > Billing > Invoice History > Export All**. Select your date range and click **Download**. The archive includes individual PDF invoices and a CSV summary.

## Tax Compliance

CloudSync Pro collects applicable sales tax for customers in US states where required and VAT for EU customers. Tax-exempt organizations can upload a valid exemption certificate at **Settings > Billing > Tax Information > Upload Exemption**.
