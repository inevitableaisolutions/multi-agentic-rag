# Billing Disputes

## Overview

If you believe a charge on your account is incorrect, CloudSync Pro provides a formal dispute resolution process. We take billing accuracy seriously and aim to resolve all disputes within 5 business days.

## Common Dispute Scenarios

### Double Billing
If you were charged twice for the same billing period, this is typically caused by a payment retry after a temporary network error. Check **Settings > Billing > Invoice History** to confirm. If you see two successful charges for the same period, file a dispute and one will be refunded automatically.

### Unauthorized Charges
If you see a charge from CloudSync Pro that you did not authorize:

1. Check whether another team member with admin access initiated the charge (e.g., plan upgrade, storage add-on)
2. Review your account activity log at **Settings > Security > Activity Log**
3. If the charge is genuinely unauthorized, file a dispute immediately and change your account password

### Incorrect Amount
If the charge amount does not match your expected plan cost, common causes include:
- Mid-cycle upgrade prorations
- Add-on charges (storage, integrations)
- Tax adjustments based on updated billing address
- User count changes (new team members added)

Review the invoice details at **Settings > Billing > Invoice History** before filing a dispute.

## How to File a Dispute

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Invoice History**
2. Find the charge in question and click **Dispute This Charge**
3. Select the dispute reason from the dropdown menu
4. Provide a brief description with any supporting details
5. Submit the dispute. You will receive a confirmation email with ticket number (format: `DSP-XXXXXX`)

Alternatively, email billing@cloudsyncpro.com with the subject line "Billing Dispute" and include your invoice number.

## Resolution Timeline

- **Acknowledgment**: Within 24 hours of submission
- **Investigation**: 3-5 business days
- **Resolution**: Refund, credit, or explanation provided within 5 business days
- **Escalation**: If unsatisfied, request escalation to a billing manager within 10 days of the resolution

## Chargebacks

We strongly recommend resolving disputes directly with CloudSync Pro before initiating a chargeback with your bank. Chargebacks can result in temporary account suspension during the investigation period. If you have already filed a chargeback, contact support@cloudsyncpro.com so we can assist the resolution process.
