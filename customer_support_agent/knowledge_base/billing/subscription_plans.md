# Subscription Plans

## Plan Overview

CloudSync Pro offers three tiers designed to scale with your team. All plans include core project management features. Paid plans unlock advanced collaboration, integrations, and support.

## Free Plan -- $0/month

- Up to 3 users per workspace
- 5 active projects
- 500 MB storage
- Basic task management (Kanban boards, lists)
- Email support (48-hour response time)
- Community forum access
- 100 API calls per day

## Pro Plan -- $29/user/month ($23/user/month billed annually)

Everything in Free, plus:

- Unlimited users per workspace
- Unlimited active projects
- 50 GB storage per workspace (additional storage at $5/10 GB/month)
- Gantt charts and timeline views
- Time tracking and reporting
- Custom fields and workflows
- Third-party integrations (Slack, Jira, GitHub, Google Drive, Dropbox)
- Priority email support (12-hour response time)
- Live chat support (business hours: Mon-Fri, 9 AM - 6 PM EST)
- 10,000 API calls per day
- Advanced permissions and role management
- Audit log (90-day retention)

## Enterprise Plan -- Custom Pricing (starting at $79/user/month)

Everything in Pro, plus:

- Dedicated account manager
- SSO/SAML authentication
- SCIM user provisioning
- Unlimited storage
- 99.9% uptime SLA with financial credits
- Custom integrations and webhook support
- 100,000 API calls per day
- Advanced security (IP allowlisting, data residency options)
- Audit log (unlimited retention)
- Phone support (24/7)
- Onboarding and training sessions
- Custom contract and invoicing terms

## How to Upgrade or Downgrade

Navigate to **Settings > Billing > Change Plan**. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated charges. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. When downgrading, features exceeding the lower plan's limits become read-only until you reduce usage.

## Annual vs Monthly Billing

Annual billing saves 20% compared to monthly. Switch to annual billing anytime from **Settings > Billing > Billing Frequency**. The prorated difference for the remaining months is applied as a credit.
