# Privacy and Data Protection

## Our Commitment to Privacy

CloudSync Pro is committed to protecting your data. We process personal data in compliance with applicable privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and SOC 2 Type II standards. Our full privacy policy is available at cloudsyncpro.com/privacy.

## Data We Collect

CloudSync Pro collects and processes the following categories of data:

- **Account data**: Name, email address, profile picture, timezone, role
- **Workspace data**: Projects, tasks, comments, file attachments, activity logs
- **Usage data**: Login timestamps, feature usage analytics, device and browser information
- **Billing data**: Payment method details (processed and stored by our PCI-compliant payment processor, Stripe)

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Usage data is collected in aggregate for product improvement and is never shared with advertisers.

## GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), CloudSync Pro acts as a data processor on behalf of your organization (the data controller). Key GDPR provisions:

- **Right to access**: Request a copy of your personal data at Settings > Account > Privacy > Download My Data
- **Right to rectification**: Update your personal information at Settings > Account > Profile
- **Right to erasure**: Request deletion of your account and personal data (see Account Deletion guide)
- **Right to data portability**: Export your data in machine-readable format (JSON/CSV) at any time
- **Data Processing Agreement (DPA)**: Available for Enterprise customers. Request a signed DPA at legal@cloudsyncpro.com

## Data Storage and Residency

- Primary data centers: AWS us-east-1 (Virginia, USA) and eu-west-1 (Ireland, EU)
- Enterprise customers can select their data residency region during workspace setup
- Available regions: US (Virginia), EU (Ireland), Asia-Pacific (Singapore)
- All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
