# Password Reset

## How to Reset Your Password

If you have forgotten your password:

1. Go to **app.cloudsyncpro.com/login**
2. Click **Forgot Password?**
3. Enter the email address associated with your account
4. Click **Send Reset Link**
5. Check your email for a message from noreply@cloudsyncpro.com (subject: "Reset Your CloudSync Pro Password")
6. Click the reset link in the email. The link expires after 1 hour.
7. Enter your new password and confirm it
8. Click **Reset Password**

The reset link can only be used once. If it has expired, request a new one.

## Password Requirements

Your new password must meet these criteria:

- Minimum 12 characters
- At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- At least one number (0-9)
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
- Cannot be any of your last 5 passwords
- Cannot contain your name or email address

## Not Receiving the Reset Email?

If you do not receive the password reset email within 5 minutes:

1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Search for emails from `noreply@cloudsyncpro.com`
3. Add `noreply@cloudsyncpro.com` to your email allowlist
4. Verify you are entering the correct email address (the one you used to sign up)
5. If using SSO, your password is managed by your identity provider -- contact your IT admin instead
6. Contact support@cloudsyncpro.com if the issue persists

## Locked Accounts

Your account is locked after 5 consecutive failed login attempts. When locked:

- You will see the message: "Account temporarily locked. Try again in 30 minutes."
- The lockout lasts 30 minutes and resets automatically
- You can bypass the lockout by resetting your password via email
- After 3 lockout events in 24 hours, the account requires manual unlocking by support. Contact support@cloudsyncpro.com with your account email.

## Changing Your Password (While Logged In)

If you know your current password and want to change it:

1. Go to **Settings > Account > Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password, then your new password
4. Click **Update Password**

All active sessions on other devices are terminated when you change your password.
