# Account Deletion

## Overview

You can delete your CloudSync Pro account at any time. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone after the grace period. Please read this guide carefully before proceeding.

## Before You Delete

Before deleting your account, consider the following:

1. **Export your data**: Use the Data Export feature to download your projects, tasks, and files. Once deleted, data cannot be recovered.
2. **Transfer ownership**: If you are the workspace Owner, transfer ownership to another admin at Settings > Workspace > Transfer Ownership. A workspace cannot exist without an owner.
3. **Cancel your subscription**: Active subscriptions are automatically cancelled upon account deletion, but no prorated refund is issued for the remaining period.
4. **Remove integrations**: Disconnect third-party integrations (Slack, GitHub, etc.) to prevent broken webhook connections.

## How to Delete Your Account

1. Go to **Settings > Account > Delete Account**
2. Enter your password to confirm your identity
3. Select a reason for leaving (optional but appreciated for product improvement)
4. Type `DELETE` in the confirmation field
5. Click **Permanently Delete My Account**

You will receive a confirmation email at your registered address.

## Grace Period

After initiating deletion:

- Your account enters a **30-day grace period**
- During this period, your account is deactivated (you cannot log in) but data is preserved
- To cancel the deletion and reactivate your account, click the link in the confirmation email or contact support@cloudsyncpro.com
- After 30 days, all data is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our servers and backups

## What Gets Deleted

- Your user profile and personal information
- All tasks, comments, and activity you created
- File attachments you uploaded
- API keys and integration tokens associated with your account
- Billing history and payment method information

## What Is Retained

- Workspace-level data (projects, shared tasks) created by other members remains intact
- Audit log entries referencing your actions are anonymized (shown as "Deleted User")
- Aggregate usage analytics (non-personally identifiable)

## Reactivation

- **Within 30-day grace period**: Click the reactivation link in your deletion confirmation email, or email support@cloudsyncpro.com with your account email
- **After 30 days**: Account cannot be reactivated. You must create a new account. Previous data is not recoverable.

## Deleting a Workspace

Only the workspace Owner can delete the entire workspace:

1. Go to **Settings > Workspace > Delete Workspace**
2. All members will be notified 7 days before deletion takes effect
3. Workspace deletion removes all projects, tasks, files, and member associations
4. This action has a 14-day grace period
