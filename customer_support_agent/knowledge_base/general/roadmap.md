# Product Roadmap

## Overview

CloudSync Pro follows a quarterly release cycle with monthly patch updates. Our roadmap is driven by customer feedback, market trends, and our product vision. This page outlines upcoming features and how to participate in early access.

## Release Schedule

- **Major releases**: Quarterly (March, June, September, December)
- **Minor releases**: Monthly (bug fixes, performance improvements, small enhancements)
- **Hotfixes**: As needed for critical security or stability issues

Release notes are published at cloudsyncpro.com/changelog and emailed to all workspace admins.

## Upcoming Features (Q2 2026)

### AI-Powered Task Suggestions -- Coming June 2026
Intelligent task creation and assignment recommendations based on project history and team workload patterns. The AI assistant will suggest task breakdowns when you create a project, estimate effort based on historical data, and recommend optimal assignees.

### Advanced Gantt Charts -- Coming May 2026
Enhanced Gantt chart view with resource leveling, critical path highlighting, baseline comparisons, and drag-to-reschedule with automatic dependency adjustment.

### Forms and Request Management -- Coming April 2026
Create custom forms for task intake, bug reports, and service requests. Forms can be shared externally (no login required) and automatically create tasks in designated projects.

## Planned Features (Q3-Q4 2026)

- **Workload management dashboard**: Visual capacity planning across team members and projects
- **Custom automations builder**: No-code rule builder (e.g., "When task status changes to Done, notify channel and archive after 30 days")
- **Guest access**: Invite external collaborators with limited permissions without consuming a paid seat
- **Portfolio view**: Aggregate multiple projects into a portfolio with cross-project reporting
- **Mobile offline mode**: Full offline support for iOS and Android with automatic sync on reconnection

## Beta Access Program

Want early access to upcoming features?

1. Go to **Settings > Account > Beta Program**
2. Toggle **Enable Beta Features**
3. New features will appear in your workspace with a "Beta" badge
4. Provide feedback using the in-app feedback widget or email beta@cloudsyncpro.com

Beta features may change before general release and are not covered by the SLA.

## Feature Requests

Submit and vote on feature requests at feedback.cloudsyncpro.com. Requests with the most votes are prioritized in our planning process. Our product team reviews all submissions monthly.
