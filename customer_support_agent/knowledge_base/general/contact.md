## Submitting a Support Ticket

For the fastest resolution, include the following in your support request:

1. **Account email** and **workspace URL**
2. **Description** of the issue with steps to reproduce
3. **Expected behavior** vs. **actual behavior**
4. **Screenshots or screen recordings** (use the in-app screenshot tool: Ctrl+Shift+S / Cmd+Shift+S)
5. **Browser and OS** information
6. **Error codes** or **error messages** if applicable

## Escalation Path

If your issue is not resolved satisfactorily:

1. **Level 1**: Initial support agent (all tickets start here)
2. **Level 2**: Senior support engineer (escalated if unresolved after 48 hours or upon request)
3. **Level 3**: Engineering team (for confirmed bugs or complex technical issues)
4. **Customer Success Manager**: Enterprise customers have a dedicated CSM reachable at csm@cloudsyncpro.com

To escalate manually, reply to your support ticket with "Please escalate" or email escalations@cloudsyncpro.com with your ticket number.
