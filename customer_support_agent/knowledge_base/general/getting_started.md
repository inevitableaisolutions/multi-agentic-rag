# Getting Started with CloudSync Pro

## Quick Start Guide

This guide will have you up and running in under 10 minutes. Follow these steps to create your first project and invite your team.

## Step 1: Sign Up (1 minute)

1. Go to **app.cloudsyncpro.com/signup**
2. Enter your name, work email, and password (or sign up with Google/Microsoft/GitHub)
3. Verify your email by clicking the confirmation link
4. Choose your workspace name and URL

## Step 2: Create Your First Project (2 minutes)

1. From your dashboard, click **+ New Project**
2. Choose a template:
   - **Blank**: Start from scratch
   - **Software Sprint**: Pre-configured with Backlog, In Progress, Review, and Done columns
   - **Marketing Campaign**: Phases for Planning, Creation, Review, and Launch
   - **Product Launch**: Timeline-based with milestones for each launch stage
3. Name your project (e.g., "Q2 Product Launch")
4. Set the start and end dates
5. Click **Create Project**

## Step 3: Add Your First Tasks (3 minutes)

Tasks are the building blocks of every project. Create them in any view:

- **Board View**: Click **+ Add Task** at the bottom of any column
- **List View**: Click **+ New Task** or press `N` on your keyboard
- **Timeline View**: Click and drag on the timeline to create a task with dates

For each task, you can set:
- **Title**: What needs to be done
- **Description**: Details, requirements, or notes (supports Markdown)
- **Assignee**: Who is responsible
- **Due date**: When it is due
- **Priority**: Low, Medium, High, or Urgent
- **Labels**: Color-coded tags for categorization (e.g., "Bug", "Feature", "Design")

## Step 4: Invite Your Team (2 minutes)

1. Click **Settings > Members > Invite**
2. Enter your teammates' email addresses
3. Assign roles: Admin (full access), Member (standard access), or Viewer (read-only)
4. Click **Send Invitations**

Alternatively, share the project directly: open the project, click **Share** (top right), and enter email addresses for project-specific access.

## Step 5: Set Up Notifications (1 minute)

Stay informed without being overwhelmed:

1. Go to **Settings > Notifications**
2. Choose what triggers notifications: task assignments, comments, mentions, status changes, due date reminders
3. Choose delivery channels: in-app, email, Slack (if connected), mobile push
4. Set quiet hours to pause notifications during off-hours

## What to Explore Next

- **Integrations**: Connect Slack, GitHub, or Google Drive at Settings > Integrations
- **Keyboard shortcuts**: Press `?` anywhere in the app to see the full list
- **Templates**: Save your project as a template for reuse at Project Settings > Save as Template
- **Dashboards**: Build a custom dashboard with widgets at Home > Customize Dashboard
- **API**: Automate workflows with the REST API at docs.cloudsyncpro.com/api
